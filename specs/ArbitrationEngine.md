# Recursive–Generative Organizational Body: ArbitrationEngine

## Name
The **ArbitrationEngine** is a subsystem of the Recursive–Generative Organizational Body. It oversees dispute resolution through structured arbitration processes.

## Input
Receives disputes, conflicts, and symbolic disagreements that require resolution.

## Function
Governs resolution through structured arbitration. It models fairness and balance in symbolic systems and ensures that conflicts are settled consistently.

## Process Loop
1. **Submit**: Disputes are submitted to the engine.
2. **Deliberate**: Structured deliberation applies defined rules.
3. **Decide**: Issue symbolic judgments.
4. **Record**: Log rulings and update system state accordingly.

## Interpretation
Dispute resolution is interpreted as restoration of equilibrium. The engine transforms conflict into structured outcomes, providing clarity and closure.

## Components
Includes a dispute arbitration module that formalizes outcomes and ensures they adhere to system guidelines.
